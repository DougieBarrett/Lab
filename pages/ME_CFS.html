The project will be investigating visual sensory and cognitive markers of ME/CFS. We will use a variety of techniques, including pupillometry, eye tracking and EEG, to compare behavioural and physiological measures of performance on visual tasks associated with difficulties and symptoms among individuals with ME/CFS.

If you have ME/CFS and are interested in taking part in the study, please keep an eye on this site, as we will be inviting participants to sessions in the George Davies Centre at the University of Leicester. We will also be recruiting participants for online studies, which can be done at home, and hope to visit support groups to discuss the study directly with sufferers, their families and clinicians.

If you have any questions, or would like to know more about the project, you can also e-mail Doug or Anosha directly using the e-mail addresses in the table above.
